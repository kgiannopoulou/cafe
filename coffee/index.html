About Us

Dine-In


Dessert Café is a popular dessert destination for college students, local residents, and travellers. We provide friendly service and offer a scrumptious array of quality desserts in the busy college Shops Plaza located around karur.


Takeout or Delivery


We carry several takeout box sizes to fit all our menu items, so come on in and grab a delectable dessert to enjoy on the go.


We also provide delivery services.
